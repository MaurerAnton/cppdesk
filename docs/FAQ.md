# Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: How do I configure cppdesk for optimal performance?

**Answer 1:** cppdesk provides extensive configuration options for performance tuning.
You can adjust video quality, codec selection, frame rate, and bitrate through the
settings interface. For best results on limited bandwidth, use adaptive bitrate with
VP9 or AV1 codec. On local networks, H264 with hardware acceleration provides lowest
latency. See the [Building Guide](BUILDING.md) and [API Reference](API_REFERENCE.md) for details.
Key settings: `video-codec`, `bitrate`, `fps`, `quality`, `scale`.

Related: See also the [Protocol Specification](PROTOCOL.md) and [Architecture](ARCHITECTURE.md) documents.
